Restaurants in Cornwall

Discover Cornwall's finest dining experiences - from seafood feasts to cozy country pubs

Cornwall's food scene has undergone a remarkable transformation in recent years, establishing the county as one of the UK's premier culinary destinations. With its abundant coastline providing the freshest seafood, fertile farmland yielding quality produce, and a new generation of innovative chefs, Cornwall offers dining experiences to rival any in the country.

From Rick Stein's famous seafood restaurants in Padstow to hidden gems tucked away in fishing villages, Cornwall's restaurants showcase the very best of local ingredients prepared with skill and passion. Whether you're seeking Michelin-starred fine dining, traditional Cornish cuisine, or relaxed beachside cafés, you'll find exceptional food throughout the county.

Dining in Cornwall: Tips for Visitors

  • Book ahead: Popular restaurants can be fully booked weeks or even months in advance, especially during peak season.
  • Try local specialties: Don't miss Cornish classics like pasties, cream teas, fresh seafood, and Cornish Yarg cheese.
  • Look for sustainable options: Many Cornish restaurants pride themselves on sustainable, locally-sourced ingredients - look for those with the Cornish Good Seafood Guide approval.
  • Consider lunch: Many fine dining restaurants offer excellent value set lunch menus that are more affordable than dinner.
  • Explore food festivals: Cornwall hosts numerous food festivals throughout the year, including the Falmouth Oyster Festival and Padstow Christmas Festival.
