About Cornwall

Cornwall is a county on England's rugged southwestern tip. It forms a peninsula encompassing wild moorland and hundreds of sandy beaches, culminating at the promontory Land's End.

With its dramatic coastline, charming fishing villages, and rich cultural heritage, Cornwall offers something for every visitor. From surfing and hiking to fine dining and historical exploration, the experiences are endless.

At CornwallThrive, we're dedicated to showcasing the very best this beautiful county has to offer, helping you discover hidden gems and create unforgettable memories.
